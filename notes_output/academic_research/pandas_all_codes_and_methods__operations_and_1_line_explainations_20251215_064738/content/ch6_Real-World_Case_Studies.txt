2. Core Concepts and Principles

A DataFrame, the cornerstone of Pandas, is a two-dimensional, labeled data structure consisting of rows and columns. Its versatility lies in its ability to accommodate columns with data of different types, making it an ideal tool for managing diverse datasets. The fundamental principles revolve around indexing, selecting, and manipulating data.
