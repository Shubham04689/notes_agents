1.2 Core Components

1.2.1 DataFrames
A two-dimensional labeled data structure, DataFrames are capable of handling columns of potentially different types. Known for their flexibility, efficiency, and intuitive nature, DataFrames are an ideal choice for working with tabular data.

1.2.2 Series
A one-dimensional labeled array-like object, Series resembles a column in a DataFrame.

1.2.3 Time Series
Useful for financial and economic data, Time Series is a Series indexed by datetime, making it ideal for time-series analysis.
